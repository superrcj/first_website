The AF1

The AF1 Lows are probably one of the most bestselling shoes ever.
Almost everyone knows what they are. The white on white sneaker has been around for decades,
and is still popular to this day. The reason being is its iconic look, which goes with any outfit.

What makes this sneaker so popular is its sleek look, and the price of it. Compared to the resell and
even retail prices of other sneakers, the AF1 Lows are considered pretty cheap at its 90$ price tag.




Its History

The Sneaker Today

The AF1 high tops were released in the 80s, which were used by NBA players. The AF1 Lows followed the release of the high tops, which soon gained tons of popularity because of its clean look. In the next few years, many new colorways would hit the market, increasing the shoe's already huge popularity.

Ever since its release in 83', Nike has released tons of new colorways. They've also released various collaborations with many different companies and celebrities. One example of these many collaborations is the Off-White Air Force 1. This is a collab with Virgil Abloh, a designer and founder of the Off-White brand. Off-White is very popular in todays streetwear.
